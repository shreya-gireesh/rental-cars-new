Bookings

To book a rental car, visit our homepage and enter your pickup and drop-off locations, along with the desired dates. Browse through our available vehicles, select your preferred car, and complete the booking process by providing your details and payment information.

The minimum age to rent a car with CarWilla is 21 years old. Drivers aged 21 to 24 may be subject to a young driver surcharge. Some car categories may have higher age requirements. Please check the terms and conditions during the booking process for specific age-related details.

CarWilla offers a diverse range of vehicles to suit your needs, including compact cars, sedans, SUVs, minivans, and luxury vehicles. Whether you're looking for an economical option or a spacious ride for your family, we have the right car for you.

Most of our rentals come with unlimited mileage, allowing you to travel as far as you need without additional charges. However, some special offers or specific vehicle categories may have mileage limits. Please check the terms and conditions of your booking for details.

You can pay for your rental car using a credit card or debit card. We accept major credit cards such as Visa and MasterCard. Payment is required at the time of booking, and a security deposit will be held on your card until the vehicle is returned in good condition.
